CREATIS - CNRS

2014 - 2015

Researcher in Image Processing I developed and implemented a new way of filtering and denoising 3D+t stacks of MR acquired images based on anisotropic diffusion. The method outperformed state-of-the-art algorithms, in particular BM3D. On a second part I also developed a pipeline for automatic atlas based segmentation of 3D CT scans based on the registration of manually segmented images. This work was integrated within the european sized project PIPER, gathering 10 partners in 5 different countries.
Villeurbanne | www.creatis.insa-lyon.fr

ERASME
